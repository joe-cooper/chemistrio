Interactive simulation that builds up hydrogen atomic orbitals, including 1s, 2s, the separate 2px, 2py and 2pz orbitals, and the five 3d orbitals, by randomly sampling electron positions from each orbital's probability distribution. Particles are colored by the sign of the wavefunction to show nodes and lobes, and their opacity can be adjusted with a slider. Below the 3D view, three small plots show thin slices of the accumulated points through the xy, xz and yz planes. The cloud can be rotated by dragging and zoomed with the scroll wheel.
