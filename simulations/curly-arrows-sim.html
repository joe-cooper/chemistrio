Curly Arrow Mechanisms — foundation prototype

Draw the curly arrows for the next step of the mechanism, then check what they actually produce. This first build covers one reaction (SN2) to prove out the engine; more mechanisms get added the same way.

1. Click a lone pair (pair of dots) or a bond to start an arrow — it turns gold. 2. Click the atom the arrow should point to, to complete it. 3. Draw all the arrows needed for this step, then press Check mechanism to see the product — right or wrong.
