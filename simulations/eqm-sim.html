Qc (current)
Kc = kf/kr
Model. Elementary kinetics assumed, so orders equal the stoichiometric coefficients: ratef = kf·∏[reactant]coeff, rater = kr·∏[product]coeff. At equilibrium the rates are equal, which rearranges to Kc = kf/kr. The plotted Qc is that same expression at the current concentrations — equal to Kc only once equilibrium is reached. Rate-constant units are illustrative.
