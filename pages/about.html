About this site

Chemistr.io is a collection of chemistry simulations and teaching resources for teachers and students to use freely.

Simulations

Simulations are organised by topic and teaching level, and come with teaching notes for additional guidance.

About the author

My name is Joe, and I am a chemistry teacher in the UK. I have found interactive simulations incredibly useful for complementing my teaching but have been frustrated by the gaps in topics covered by them. I decided to have a punt at making my own and host them here for others to use.
