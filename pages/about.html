For Teachers

About this site

This hub gathers interactive chemistry simulations and teaching materials in one place. The simulations are designed to make abstract ideas — particle motion, equilibrium, kinetics — visible and adjustable, so students can build intuition by experimenting rather than memorising.

How to use it

  • Project a simulation during a lesson and adjust parameters live to prompt discussion.
  • Set a simulation as a starter or plenary — ask students to predict before you change a variable.
  • Share the link directly with students for independent study and revision.
  • Download worksheets to accompany the relevant simulation.
