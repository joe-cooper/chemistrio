About this site

Chemistr.io is a collection of chemistry simulations and teaching resources for teachers and students to use freely.

▶ About the author

I am a chemistry teacher in the UK. I have found interactive simulations incredibly useful for complementing my teaching but wished for more to cover the gaps in other topics. I decided to have a punt at making my own and host them here for others to use. If you have suggestions or feedback, or just want to show support, please feel free to use the links below.
